About Us

What we are? hear from us

ISKCON is known as Hare Krishnas religious organization. They have a huge collection of popular Hare Krishna recipes. It contains traditional recipes commonly cooked in India and western and continental recipes. They are storing the recipe information in EXCEL sheets which is used for sharing information and cooking methods with everyone. By sharing an EXCEL sheet it's a confusing process for everyone to get proper information of particular recipes also there is the issue of security and tracking on the excel sheet. Also by increasing number of recipes file size and loading time will get increase so our application is will help overcome that problems along with storing data in a structural and secured format. We aim to build web application for storing all the recipes by applying various categories on data so that anyone from all over the world can follow traditional recipes which will help to maintaining health which is known as “Satvik-Bhojan” along with steps for every recipe. Django which contains many libraries and bootstrap for attractive UI designing are the tech-stack. User can also get real time prices of every ingredient which can help user to calculate total cost as per serving size and total number of serving .The main aim of ISCON is to systematically propagate spiritual knowledge to society at large and to educate all people in the techniques of spiritual life which can be fulfilled by spreading knowledge through contents our idea is to show main shlokas , Gita , Ramayana content on website along with recipes information which will develop interest in spirituality of life.
